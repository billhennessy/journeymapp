JourneyMapp™

JourneyMapp™ is a simple tool for mapping a person's journey through an experience.

That journey could be with a trip, a brand, a program, an employer . . . anything.

This app builds on the work presented in the book Designing for Growth: a design thinking toolkit for managers by Jeanne Liedtka and Tim Ogilvie.

3 Simple Steps

Step 1: Define the Journey
Step 2: Record Experiences
Step 3: Upload photos or videos of the experience
